Detailed Description

Encapsulation of the Orientation of the Faces of a Box.

This class encapsulates the orientation of the faces of a Box by providing an ordering of each of the faces of a Box in BL_SPACEDIM dimensions. This allows iterating over all the faces of a Box. The ordering first traverses the BL_SPACEDIM low sides from direction 0 .. BL_SPACEDIM-1 and then the BL_SPACEDIM high sides from direction 0 .. BL_SPACEDIM-1.
